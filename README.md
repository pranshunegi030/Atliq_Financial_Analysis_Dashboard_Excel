# atliq_financial_analysis_excel

**Project Overview :**
This project focuses on Profit & Loss (P&L) analysis for AtliQ Hardware using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to evaluate financial performance across fiscal years, markets, sub-zones, and months while monitoring profitability through Gross Margin and Gross Margin Percentage (GM%).

The reports provide insights into revenue growth, cost management, and profitability trends to support financial decision-making.

**Business Problem :**
Business leaders require visibility into financial performance across different regions and time periods to answer key questions such as:

How has revenue grown over time?
What is the relationship between Net Sales and Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)?
Which markets generate the highest profits?
Which regions maintain the strongest gross margins?
How does profitability vary across fiscal years and months?

This project addresses these questions through interactive financial reports built in Excel.

**Reports Included :**
**1. P&L Report by Fiscal Years :**
Provides a high-level financial summary across fiscal years 2019–2021.
Insights :
Net Sales increased from $87.5M in 2019 to $598.9M in 2021.
Gross Margin grew from $36.2M to $218.2M.
Net Sales increased by over 204% between 2020 and 2021.
Gross Margin increased by approximately 198% during the same period.

Report Link: https://github.com/pranshunegi030/atliq_financial_analysis_excel/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Year%20Report%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf

**2. P&L Report for Markets :**
Analyzes profitability across countries using Net Sales, COGS, Gross Margin, and GM%.
Insights :
India generated the highest Net Sales at $161.3M.
USA generated $87.8M in sales.
New Zealand recorded the highest GM% at approximately 48.2%.
Japan and the United Kingdom also demonstrated strong profitability.

Report Link: https://github.com/pranshunegi030/atliq_financial_analysis_excel/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Report%20for%20Markets.pdf

**3. P&L Report for Sub-Zones :**
Compares Gross Margin Percentage across business sub-zones and fiscal years.
Insights :
ANZ consistently maintained the highest profitability levels.
ROA and SE regions showed stable Gross Margin performance.
India generated high revenue but operated at comparatively lower GM%.
Profitability trends can be monitored quarterly for each sub-zone.

Report Link: https://github.com/pranshunegi030/atliq_financial_analysis_excel/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Report%20for%20Sub%20Zones.pdf

**4. P&L Report by Months :**
Provides month-wise financial performance analysis.
Insights :
Significant growth in Net Sales is visible across fiscal years.
Monthly Gross Margin trends help identify seasonal business performance.
The report enables tracking of profitability throughout the fiscal cycle.

Report Link: https://github.com/pranshunegi030/atliq_financial_analysis_excel/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20By%20Months.pdf
